About Me

CAR ENTHUSIAST AND GAMER
I'm a car enthusiast and gamer, driven by a lifelong passion for automobiles and digital adventures. From my early days playing with matchbox cars to racing in legal drag events with my two beloved Honda Civics, cars have always been my fascination. Before settling in Canada, I honed my skills as a Customer Service Representative, transitioning smoothly to remote work as a Senior Recruiter for a U.S Company in 2016.

CAREER SHIFT
Despite my background in recruitment, I've embarked on a new journey in software development, drawn to the challenge and excitement it offers. Gaming has been my sanctuary, providing both entertainment and an avenue for creative expression. Managing a private server for Grand Theft Auto V introduced me to script development, sparking a newfound interest in coding and customization.

CODING JOURNEY
Navigating through lines of code, troubleshooting errors for hours on end, and tweaking scripts to perfection have become my passion projects. Despite lacking a formal IT background, I've tried exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, crafting unique user interfaces and enhancing player experiences.

WORK-LIFE BALANCE
Balancing family responsibilities, work, and education in a new country has presented its challenges, but with unwavering faith, teamwork, and determination, we've persevered. While my gaming hours may be limited now, I find solace in knowing that every endeavor brings me closer to my goals and contributes to a fulfilling life for my loved ones.
